## Suggested Evaluation Criteria

- A4 print readability at your target font scale.
- Ability to understand full control flow without cross-referencing too often.
- Visual continuity of loops and fallback logic.
- Space efficiency versus explanatory clarity.

## Quick Guidance

- Choose **3A** if you want full detail and natural continuity across two side-by-side panels.
- Choose **3B** for one-page, one-diagram simplicity.
- Choose **3C** when you want a clean narrative: main flow plus focused retry details.
- Choose **3D** when space is most constrained.
